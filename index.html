My favorite Vacation spot is Rishikesh and Haridwar

Rishikesh and Haridwar are beloved destinations for a variety of reasons, and their charm and appeal attract visitors from all over the world.

Rishikesh and Haridwar serve as gateways to the Himalayan region. Travelers often use these cities as starting points for journeys into the Himalayas, whether for trekking, pilgrimage, or exploring the scenic beauty of the mountains.
